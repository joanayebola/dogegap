Curriculum

At Doge Gap Homeschool, we provide an exciting and comprehensive curriculum that stimulates a love for learning while ensuring a strong foundation in core subjects. Our approach combines elements of traditional education with innovative teaching methodologies, creating a dynamic and engaging educational experience.

We offer specialized subjects and programs that enrich our students' learning journey:

  • STEM: Our science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) program fosters curiosity and critical thinking through hands-on experiments and collaborative projects.
  • Arts: We celebrate creativity and self-expression with a diverse arts curriculum that includes visual arts, music, and performing arts.
  • Foreign Languages: Students have the opportunity to explore the world through language learning, expanding their cultural awareness and communication skills.
  • Character Education: We place great emphasis on fostering positive values and character development, nurturing well-rounded individuals who are compassionate and socially responsible.

With this holistic curriculum, we prepare our students for a lifetime of learning, personal growth, and success.

Assessment and Grading

At Doge Gap Homeschool, we believe in comprehensive assessment practices that focus on student growth, progress, and individualized learning. We use a variety of assessment methods to evaluate student performance and provide valuable feedback to both students and parents.

We employ the following assessment and grading practices:

  • Portfolios: Students maintain portfolios that showcase their work, including projects, assignments, and creative pieces. Portfolios provide a holistic view of student progress and allow for self-reflection and growth.
  • Projects: We emphasize project-based learning, where students complete in-depth projects to demonstrate their understanding of concepts and skills. Projects encourage critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.
  • Tests and Quizzes: We incorporate periodic tests and quizzes to assess students' knowledge and understanding of specific subjects or topics. These assessments help identify areas of strength and areas that may need additional focus.
  • Student-Led Conferences: We believe in involving students in their own learning process. Student-led conferences provide an opportunity for students to showcase their work, reflect on their progress, and engage in meaningful discussions about their learning journey with their parents or guardians.

We provide regular feedback to parents to keep them informed about their child's progress. Communication channels include:

  • Progress Reports: We issue periodic progress reports that outline students' academic achievements, strengths, areas for improvement, and overall progress.
  • Parent-Teacher Communication: We encourage open communication between parents and teachers. Regular meetings, emails, and other forms of communication are utilized to discuss student performance, address concerns, and collaborate on strategies to support the student's learning journey.
  • Online Platforms: We utilize online platforms or learning management systems to provide real-time access to student assignments, grades, and feedback. This allows parents to stay updated and involved in their child's learning process.

By employing these assessment and grading practices and maintaining open lines of communication, we aim to provide a comprehensive understanding of each student's progress and support their continued growth and success.

Frequently Asked Questions

Enrollment

1. What are the enrollment requirements?
Enrollment requirements may vary depending on the grade level. Generally, we require proof of age, completed application forms, and any relevant academic records or transcripts.
2. Can I enroll my child at any time during the year?
Yes, we accept enrollments throughout the year, subject to availability. Please contact us to check for space availability and to discuss the enrollment process.

Tuition and Fees

1. What is the tuition for the homeschool program?
Tuition varies based on the grade level and the specific program. Please contact us for detailed information about tuition fees.
2. Are there any additional fees?
In addition to tuition, there may be fees for textbooks, learning materials, field trips, and other extracurricular activities. These fees are communicated to parents in advance.
3. What payment options are available?
We offer various payment options, including monthly, quarterly, or annual payment plans. Our admissions team can provide you with more information regarding payment options.

Admissions Process

1. What is the admissions process?
The admissions process typically involves submitting an application form, providing necessary documentation, attending an interview or assessment (if required), and completing the enrollment paperwork once accepted. Please reach out to us for detailed information about the admissions process.
2. Is there an admissions deadline?
We accept applications throughout the year, subject to availability. However, to secure a spot, we encourage prospective students to apply as early as possible.

Transportation

1. Do you provide transportation services?
As a homeschool program, we do not provide transportation services. Families are responsible for arranging transportation for their children to and from our program and any off-site activities or events.
