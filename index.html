About Us

Doge Gap has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 2005. From the very beginning, our aim has been to provide a transformative educationsl experience for students in our community. Starting with a small group of dedicated educators , we embarked on a journey to create a school that would prioritize academic excellence and holistic development.

Over the years, Doge Gap has evolved and grown, continously adapting to meet the changing needs of our studebts. We have embraced innovative teaching methodologies, integrating technology and experiential learning into our curriculum. Our dedicated team of educators has always been at the forefront of educational trends, ensuring that our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in an ever-changing world

Our Services

Enrichment Programs

Alongside core subjects, we offer a range of enrichment programs such as art, music, physical education, coding and more. These programs allow students to explore their passions and develop well-rounded skills.

Individual Attention

Each student recieves individualized attention from experienced educators. Our low student-to-teacher ratio ensures personalized support, guidance and feedback to maximize learning outcomes.

Engaging Resources

We provide a wide range of engaging resources, including interactive online materials, educational apps and hands-on learning tools. Theses resources enhance the learning experience and make it enjoyable for students.

Flexible Scheduling

We offer flexible scheduling options to accomodate students' unique routines and family commitments. Whether you prefer a traditional schedule or a more flexible approach, we can tailor the learning timetable to suit your needs.
